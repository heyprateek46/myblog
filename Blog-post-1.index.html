You need to relax more 😌

Published on January 16, 2025

Save your time - it is the most important resource that you can’t buy more of. I was recently listening to one of the podcasts featuring Naval where he was talking about why he believes that we should live life like a lion.

Naval’s post on X

Eat like a lion, work like a lion, feel like a lion, live like a lion.

— Naval (@naval) March 6, 2022

What I realized while listening to Naval was pretty fascinating. He suggests that we should live our life like a lion or an athlete - most of the time should be spent resting, lazying around,and doing whatever we find entertaining or interesting.

Then once in a while, you find something that you get obsessed with, just pour your 100% to it and go at it with full force.

Resting helps you recharge, keep you ready for your next challenge or endeavour, just like a lion who sleeps for most of the time and when hungry, hunts and eat.

It’s always advantageous to rather be recharged and ready for your next intellectual or physical obsession - than putting yourself on the grinder like wake up at this time, eat this, don’t eat that, do push-ups, go for a jog, run, be productive, socialize and live a regimented mechanical life. (Wait - but this is what all the folks on the internet are preaching about - ahh, this is for another post)

He suggests to rather live a free life. No calendar, no reaching anywhere on time, no obligations to anyone, don’t have to answer people - just live free.

Anyways, don’t we all run behind chasing freedom?
